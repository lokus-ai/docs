## What you can do

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Write in markdown" icon="pencil">
		A block editor with slash commands, wiki links, callouts, LaTeX math, Mermaid diagrams, and 20+ syntax-highlighted languages.
	</Card>
	<Card title="See how ideas connect" icon="random">
		Graph view shows every link between your notes in 2D, 3D, or force-directed layouts. Click any node to navigate.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Think visually" icon="document">
		Infinite canvas powered by TLDraw. Drag notes, draw shapes, and lay out ideas spatially.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Build databases" icon="list-format">
		Turn frontmatter into structured views — tables, boards, galleries. Filter, sort, and write formulas across your notes.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
